Soup


Description

A filling soup that's good for you.

If chicken soup is for the soul, what is cheese soup for?

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Melted Cheese
  • Cream
  • Broccoli
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Butter
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Peel onions and carrots
  2. Cut broccoli into small pieces
  3. Grate melted cheese
  4. Preheat several oils on frying pan
  5. Fry onions, carrots, and flour for two minutes
  6. Boil the broth and transfer frying pan contents into broth
  7. Add broccoli and boil for five minutes
  8. Add grated melted cheese and cream and stir until dissolved
  9. Bon appetit!
