Buckwheat


Description

Buckwheat is one of the healthiest carbs out there. You can add anything to it!

This recipe uses an oven whereas buckwheat is usually prepared by boiling.

Ingredients

  • Buckwheat
  • Onions
  • Red Peppers
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Wash the buckwheat
  2. Peel onions, red peppers, and carrots
  3. Cut onions and peppers into cubes, grate carrots
  4. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C and prepare a baking tray
  5. Fry onions and carrots on medium heat on a hot oiled pan for about five minutes
  6. Add peppers and salt and pepper. Stir and cook for two more minutes
  7. Add buckwheat to the frying pan, mix with vegetables and transfer all to baking tray
  8. Fill the tray with water, cover with foil, and bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes
  9. Bon appetit!
