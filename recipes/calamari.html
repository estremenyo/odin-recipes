Calamari


Description

A classic seafood staple.

Serves 4-5 people.

Ingredients

  • Calamari
  • Melted Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Melted Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cucucmber
  • Leafy Greens

Steps

  1. Place calamari in boiling salted water (1kg calamari, 2L water, 20g salt)
  2. Return to boil and boil on low heat for five minutes
  3. Remove, cool, and chop calamari into strips
  4. Peel and chop garlic. Mix with mayonnaise
  5. Chop cucumber into strips and finely chop eggs, grate cheese, finely chop the greenery
  6. Place the calamari, cucumber, eggs, cheese, greenery, and mayonnaise with garlic into a salad bowl
  7. Mix carefully
  8. Bon appetit!
